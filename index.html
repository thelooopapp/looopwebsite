Why Looop?

Because caring from a distance should bring peace of mind, not added stress.

Daily Reassurance: Know your loved ones are safe and going about their day, even without the details.

Wellness Snapshots: Optional updates like steps, heart rate, or sleep patterns, so you can stay in the loop if you'd like.

Weather Awareness: Get alerts for storms, flood warnings, extreme heat, or cold that might affect them.

Moments That Matter: Receive gentle notifications when something important changes.
