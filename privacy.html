Looop Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 11, 2025

Looop is dedicated to safeguarding your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, disclosure, transfer, storage, and protection of personal information collected through our products and services. It also details your choices concerning your information.

This policy applies to Looop, LLC. ("Looop", "we", "our", or "us") websites, mobile applications, help centers, and other associated services we provide ("Services").

Before accessing or using our Products and/or Services, please ensure that you have read this Policy and understand how we collect, store, use, and disclose your personal information as described herein.

1. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide and improve our Services:

Information You Provide to Us

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and phone number. Authentication is secured using Firebase Authentication with Two-Factor Authentication (2FA).

Information We Collect When You Use Our Services

Health Data (via Apple HealthKit): With your consent, we collect health-related data, including but not limited to steps, heart rate, sleep patterns, and other HealthKit metrics. This data is securely stored on our servers and is used solely to share with the friends and family members you designate.

Location Data: We collect your location data both while the app is in use and in the background (if permission is granted). This data is used exclusively to share your real-time location with the friends and family members you designate.

Information Collected Through Third-Party Services

Financial Data (via Plaid): Looop integrates with Plaid to notify you of large transactions in your connected bank accounts. We do not store any banking credentials or account data; all interactions occur directly through Plaid.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, personalize, and improve our Services.
  • Facilitate sharing of health and location data with your designated contacts.
  • Notify you of significant financial transactions via Plaid.
  • Communicate with you, including sending service-related notices.

We do not use your personal information for advertising purposes.

3. Disclosure of Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except in the following circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: We may share your information with third parties if you give us explicit consent to do so.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

4. Your Choices About Your Information

You have control over your personal information:

  • Account Information: You may update or correct your account information at any time by accessing your account settings.
  • Health and Location Data: You can revoke permissions for HealthKit and Location access at any time through your device settings.
  • Data Deletion: To request deletion of your account and all associated data, please contact us via our website's contact form. We will process your request within 5 business days.

5. Our Policy Towards Children

Looop does not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 13. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected such information, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

6. How We Protect Your Information

We implement industry-standard security measures, including encrypted storage and secure authentication (2FA), to protect your data from unauthorized access or disclosure.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you through the Services or by other means to provide you the opportunity to review the changes before they become effective.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at:

Email: info@TheLooopApp.com
Website: TheLooopApp.com

This Privacy Policy is designed to provide you with a clear understanding of how we handle your personal information. Your privacy is of utmost importance to us, and we are committed to protecting it.
